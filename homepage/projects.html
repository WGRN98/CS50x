Project CS50

Recently I have embarked on this CS50 course as a personal project in my free time to develop computer science skills and have access to new opportunities in the future.

Even though I have a degree in Physics Engineering it had a much bigger focus on theoretical physics than on engineering. With my departure from physics more and more, getting into the engineering side and with my job as a telecommunications engineer there was a need to develop computer science and programming skills.

In this course I have developeed skills in languages that where not in the curriculum (only having learnt C and C++), more specifically Python, SQL, Javascript, HTML, CSS.

The most useful skill for me in this point in time was SQL and my favourite problem was Fiftyville. It was well designed and fun.

For the future I seek to continue with other CS50 courses, primarilly for AI and Databases.
